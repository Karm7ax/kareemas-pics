Hi!! My name's Kareema, and I love taking photos! I am a freshman at MSU and majoring in Games and Interactive Media. One of my first big girl birthday gifts was a Kodak PIXPRO FZ43 camera. It's was small, but mighty! I still use it a lot today, and it is what really got me into taking photos. Taking photos is just a small hobby for me, I'm definetely not as into it as some people I have met. Not all my photos are necessarily "good", but I have fun doing it.

My History with This Hobby...!

Starting Out!

So I got a camera when I was around eleven. I used to take it with me during family events, trips, and even school sometimes. I really just took pictures of what I thought looked cool and pretty. There was a point where my family moved, I missed placed my camera, and forgot about it. I had a lot of gaps where I lost it and forgot all about it. It wasn't until the beginning of high school that I found it again. I started taking it with me everywhere, and once aI started working I bought an importer so I could finally have and send photos on my phone. As time went on I got into this rabbit hole of buying used/broken cameras. I would buy them off Ebay or estate sales! Some of them even came with SD cards that the previous owners used. I would be so mad if I forgot my SD card in my cameera while seeling it. I also have a camcorder that I fixed, but I barely use it unless I make a mini vlog. I currently have 3 cameras, and SD cards with 1k+ photos on almost each!!

What do I take pictures of?

I am one of those annoyingly nostalgic people. I love taking pictures with my friends and family when we hangout. Last year I started a photo album of all the cool people and things I've been doing at college just to reminisce when I'm older. I also really enjoy taking photos of nature. I think that the world is sooo beautiful and it's one of my favorite things to capture.
